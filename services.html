Explore Our Healthcare Services

At Eloheh Healthcare, we offer a wide range of services to promote health equity, simplify clinical workflows, and ensure better patient outcomes. From virtual care to in-person outreach, our services support both providers and patients across communities.

Core Services:

  1. Telehealth Consultations – HIPAA-compliant virtual visits for patients in rural and urban areas.
  2. Mobile Wellness Clinics – Our vans deliver basic care and screening services directly to underserved areas.
  3. Healthcare Training Workshops – Training local staff in emergency response, chronic care, and community wellness.

Service Features:

  • Multilingual support available for Spanish, French, and Mandarin
  • Affordable service packages for nonprofit organizations and schools
  • Custom scheduling options including evenings and weekends
